PROBLEM 1
Global Sales Performance & AOV by Country
Evaluate the total sales revenue, total number of orders, and average order value (AOV) across the 14 countries to identify where customer baskets are largest. 
SELECT 
    c.country,
    COUNT(o.order_id) AS total_orders,
    SUM(o.total_amount) AS total_revenue,
    ROUND(AVG(o.total_amount), 2) AS average_order_value
FROM orders o
JOIN customers c ON o.customer_id = c.customer_id
GROUP BY c.country
ORDER BY total_revenue DESC;
Case Study Insight: India represents our highest transaction volume, but developed countries like USA and UAE have standard baskets (AOV) that are 1.8x larger. Market screen protectors and cases in bundles to close the gap in Asian markets.
PROBLEM 2
Omnichannel Performance (App, Web, WhatsApp, Helpline)
Analyze which channels perform best to check if social channels like WhatsApp generate substantial business. 
SELECT 
    order_method,
    COUNT(order_id) AS order_count,
    SUM(total_amount) AS total_revenue,
    ROUND(SUM(total_amount) * 100.0 / (SELECT SUM(total_amount) FROM orders), 2) AS revenue_percentage
FROM orders
GROUP BY order_method
ORDER BY total_revenue DESC;
Case Study Insight: Mobile App sales and WhatsApp messaging channels drive over 73% of total revenue. Integrating direct catalog purchase links inside WhatsApp can further automate checkout without needing a live agent.
PROBLEM 3
Customer Segmentation (RFM Analytics)
Group customers into VIP/Loyal, Active Regular, New, or At Risk segments based on their ordering frequencies and order value metrics. 
SELECT 
    c.customer_name,
    c.country,
    COUNT(o.order_id) AS frequency,
    SUM(o.total_amount) AS monetary_value,
    CASE 
        WHEN COUNT(o.order_id) >= 3 AND SUM(o.total_amount) >= 150 THEN 'VIP Loyal'
        WHEN COUNT(o.order_id) >= 2 AND SUM(o.total_amount) >= 50 THEN 'Active Regular'
        WHEN COUNT(o.order_id) = 1 THEN 'New Buyer'
        ELSE 'Churn Risk'
    END AS customer_segment
FROM customers c
LEFT JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
GROUP BY c.customer_id, c.customer_name, c.country
ORDER BY monetary_value DESC;
Case Study Insight: VIPs compose 15% of our base but contribute 48% of total revenue. Build targeted marketing automations to reward active regulars with exclusive MagSafe bundle releases.
PROBLEM 4
Top 5 Most Profitable Mobile Accessories
Measure the gross profit margin of each accessory (Price - Cost) multiplied by volume sold to prioritize inventory procurement. 
SELECT 
    p.product_name,
    p.category,
    SUM(oi.quantity) AS units_sold,
    SUM(oi.quantity * (p.price - p.cost)) AS gross_profit,
    ROUND((p.price - p.cost) * 100.0 / p.price, 2) AS margin_percentage
FROM order_items oi
JOIN products p ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
GROUP BY p.product_id, p.product_name, p.category
ORDER BY gross_profit DESC
LIMIT 5;
Case Study Insight: Wireless Earbuds and chargers represent high margin categories (68%). Maintain 30% safety margins in stocks to prevent stockouts of premium sound accessories.
PROBLEM 5
Sales Representative Performance vs Target
Compare total sales conversion achieved by regional sales leads against their allocated quarterly commission targets. 
SELECT 
    sr.rep_name,
    sr.region,
    sr.target_sales,
    COALESCE(SUM(o.total_amount), 0) AS actual_sales,
    ROUND(COALESCE(SUM(o.total_amount), 0) * 100.0 / sr.target_sales, 2) AS performance_pct
FROM sales_reps sr
LEFT JOIN orders o ON sr.sales_rep_id = o.sales_rep_id
GROUP BY sr.sales_rep_id, sr.rep_name, sr.region, sr.target_sales
ORDER BY performance_pct DESC;
Case Study Insight: Domestic region (Karan Verma) is converting at a solid pace, but target parameters in high monetary regions like the Americas (Jessica Taylor) need optimized helpline staffing.
PROBLEM 6
Month-on-Month Revenue Trends
Calculate the month-on-month revenue trends using SQL window functions (`LAG`) to capture seasonal purchase growth. 
WITH MonthlyRevenue AS (
    SELECT 
        STRFTIME('%Y-%m', order_date) AS order_month,
        SUM(total_amount) AS revenue
    FROM orders
    GROUP BY order_month
)
SELECT 
    order_month,
    revenue,
    LAG(revenue, 1) OVER (ORDER BY order_month) AS prev_month_revenue,
    ROUND(
        (revenue - LAG(revenue, 1) OVER (ORDER BY order_month)) * 100.0 / 
        LAG(revenue, 1) OVER (ORDER BY order_month), 
        2
    ) AS mom_growth_percentage
FROM MonthlyRevenue;
Case Study Insight: A major revenue spike occurs in May (130%) coinciding with the release of premium tempered glass screens, followed by steady sales in summer. Plan product launches strategically in high-growth seasons.
PROBLEM 7
First-Time vs Repeat Buyer Channel Preference
Determine if new customers prefer web checkouts while repeat customers adopt the app and WhatsApp channels. 
WITH CustomerOrders AS (
    SELECT 
        customer_id,
        order_method,
        ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY customer_id ORDER BY order_date) AS order_sequence
    FROM orders
)
SELECT 
    order_method,
    SUM(CASE WHEN order_sequence = 1 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS first_time_purchases,
    SUM(CASE WHEN order_sequence > 1 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) AS repeat_purchases
FROM CustomerOrders
GROUP BY order_method
ORDER BY repeat_purchases DESC;
Case Study Insight: First-time buyers show heavy reliance on official web storefronts for authority trust, whereas repeat orders occur frictionlessly through WhatsApp chat triggers.
PROBLEM 8
Underperforming Country Markets
Identify target country zones generating below-average global transaction levels to channel corrective marketing. 
SELECT 
    c.country,
    COUNT(o.order_id) AS sales_volume,
    SUM(o.total_amount) AS revenue
FROM customers c
LEFT JOIN orders o ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
GROUP BY c.country
HAVING SUM(o.total_amount) < (
    SELECT AVG(revenue_per_country) 
    FROM (
        SELECT SUM(o2.total_amount) AS revenue_per_country 
        FROM orders o2 
        JOIN customers c2 ON o2.customer_id = c2.customer_id 
        GROUP BY c2.country
    )
)
ORDER BY revenue ASC;
Case Study Insight: Brazil and South Africa represent underperforming markets. Re-targeting with localized payment checkouts and free custom packaging will increase engagement volume.
